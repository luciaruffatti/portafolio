LUCIA RUFFATTI

Chucho Reyes

This project consisted in creating a publication about Chucho Reyes that showcased his most famous artworks throughout his life. His biography was written by Arturo López Rodrigo.

Jesús Reyes Ferreira, (1880-1977) born José de Jesús Benjamín Buenaventura de los Reyes y Ferreira and also known as Chucho Reyes, was a self-taught artist and antiques/art collector and vendor. As a self-taught painter, his works are relatively simple and often are dismissed as folk painting but they were and his aesthetics were praised by famous artists and architects at the time.
